St. Catharines isn’t loud about itself. That’s the point. It’s the kind of place where your routine upgrades quietly — better mornings, calmer streets, and the kind of neighbourhoods that don’t need to “try” to feel good.

## A simple way to test the “fit”
If you can do three things easily, you’ll probably like it here:
- walk to coffee without planning your life around parking
- get to the highway without chaos
- have a few places that feel like “yours”
